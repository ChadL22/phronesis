On Phronesis

Wisdom, not just knowledge.

Phronesis is a Greek term for practical wisdom—the intellectual virtue of knowing not only the right course of action, but understanding why it is right. Rooted in Aristotelian philosophy, it emphasizes sound judgment acquired through lived experience and the careful consideration of both means and outcomes.

In technology, law, and policy, practical wisdom means moving beyond abstract principles to consider real-world implications. It means asking not just whether we can regulate, but whether we should, and if so, how. We write and share content that lives up to this ideal, applying practical wisdom to the governance challenges shaping our technological future.
